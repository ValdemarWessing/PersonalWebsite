Vandpyt

Project type: Short film (9 min).
Co-directors: Mads Bonde and Maria-May Brown.

Vandpyt is a short film about a little girl trying to connect with her father. I composed the music for the film.

The film won best animated film at Odense Film Festival and was shortlisted for the Robert Award 2025 for Best Short Film.
