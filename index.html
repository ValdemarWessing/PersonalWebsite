about me

I’m a Medialogy student working with code, interactive systems, and user interaction. I like building things and experimenting across different mediums - from software and games to gesture-based systems and physical setups. Alongside my technical work, I also make music and have composed soundtracks for multiple short films, as well as working with painting and visual expression. Most of what I do sits between structure and experimentation, where I try to turn technical ideas into something tangible and engaging.
